06. $\forall n > 1, \text{we have}$ $$ \begin{equation} \begin{split} {n \choose 2}+{n-1 \choose 2} &= \frac{n!}{2!(n-2)!}+\frac{(n-1)!}{2!(n-1-2)!} \\ &= \frac{n(n-1)!}{2}+\frac{(n-1)(n-2)}{2} \\ &= \bigg(\frac{1}{2}\bigg)(n)(n-1)+\bigg(\frac{1}{2}\bigg)(n-1)(n-2) \\ &= \bigg(\frac{1}{2}\bigg)(n-1)\big[n+(n-2)\big] \\ &= \bigg(\frac{1}{2}\bigg)(n-1)(2n-2) \\ &= \bigg(\frac{1}{2}\bigg)(2n^2-2n-2n+2) \\ &= \bigg(\frac{1}{\color{grey}{\cancel{2}}}\bigg)(\color{grey}{\cancel{2}})(n^2-2n+1) \\ &= n^2-2n+1 \\ &= (n-1)^2. \\ &\ \square \end{split} \end{equation} $$
